Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Children aged 3-6 grow rapidly, learning social, motor, and language skills through play, exploration, creativity, and early education experiences.

Elementary School

Elementary school builds basic skills, fosters creativity, teaches teamwork, and nurtures curiosity, preparing children for future learning and growth.

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle years, from 10 to 16, are transformative, involving growth, self-discovery, friendships, challenges, and support shaping future aspirations.
